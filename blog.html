About Tulip Ivory
- A residents view

Tulip Ivory is an eye-catching residential project in Gurgaon, and what sets it apart is its story of innovation and stunning structures. Located in Sector 70, Gurgaon, this plush residential complex offers Duplex House, Penthouse and 4bhk apartments, with a total of 48 grand villas and six towers of 4bhk flats. This residential project is developed by Tulip Infratech with a belief that everyone deserves a better life. Tulip Ivory provides exceptional life experiences, security and value. With innovation, quality, and sustainability at the core of the developer’s work and a unique customer centric approach has put us on the league of the best and most trusted developers in the business. This property has seen the highest rate of capital appreciation and have given our customers an asset to own for life.

The build-up area of these flats ranges from 2400-7500 sqft. The location is the project’s USP as it offers all the luxury amenities of life one can think of. This Ready to move in Project in Gurgaon is well-connected to Airport, Railway Stations, bus stops various international schools, hospitals, shopping malls and city clubs.

Getting up to dizzying modern architect’s heights by exploring the view from the own balcony is the USP of the society which has a breath-taking view of the Aravali Range. The central walk pavilion is tastefully laid out with seasonal blooms, lot of champa greens, fairy lights, and charming seats. Whether you look up to the blue or look down upon the green, the luxury of this home is enhanced by the bounties of nature. The society is also a great choice for senior citizen with a great security. Someone who looks for a home outside of the city’s hustle bustle, can definitely choose this property located primarily and has a healthy surrounding.

The apartment is well constructed and beautifully planned with all necessary top amenities such as Palm Court, Swimming Pool, Skating Ring, Tennis Court, Cricket Practice Pitch, Sand Pit, Barbecue Corner, Lazy Lawn Corner, Water Fall, Party/Event Lawn, Medical Room Kids Play Area and Basketball Court. The club house is one of best in this area. Ample walking area outside as well as in the whole basement make it more breathable. On maintenance point of view it will come on little higher side but its worth spending.
